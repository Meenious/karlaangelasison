PROJECTS

PROJECT I.

HOTEL GUEST ROOM

A playful and vibrant hotel that speaks to young tourists and families are their target with an innovative integration of natural resources that will exhibit sustainability but not neglecting maintenance.

PROJECT II.

Hotel Lobby

A space that reflects a vibrant, young, and fresh design that fits the Instagram curated culture of young professionals which also executes a chic, innovative, fun, and trendy style.

PROJECT III.

BIOPHILIC LUXURY LOBBY

A harmonious blend of nature and elegance, this lobby features rich green and gold tones, vertical gardens, and a Zen-inspired rock garden. Bamboo accents and plush seating create a sophisticated yet tranquil atmosphere.

PROJECT IV.

MODERN FARMHOUSE LIVING & DINING AREA

This project captures the essence of farmhouse style with warm wood tones, neutral palettes, and rustic yet refined decor. Featuring classic furniture and natural textures, the design creates an inviting, modern space that balances simplicity with elegance, ideal for contemporary living.

PROJECT V.

CONTEMPORARY- SCANDINAVIAN LIVING & DINING

This open-concept space blends minimalism contemporary with Scandinavian warmth. The kitchens sleek appliances and textured backsplash lead into a cozy dining area with a black round table and plush chairs. In the living room, a charcoal sofa with earth toned pillows and nature inspired artwork create a serene, inviting atmosphere, framed by large sliding doors with lush outdoor views.

PROJECT VI.

LIVING AREA

A harmonious and functional space that reflects the homeowners style, improves comfort and relaxation, maximizes natural light and airflow, incorporates sustainable practices, and meets the needs of both daily living and occasional entertainment.

PROJECT VII.

LIVING AREA REDECOR

The redesigned space transforms an initially sparse and uninviting room into a vibrant, sophisticated area. Centered around a bold red sofa, the space is enhanced with abstract wall art, strategic lighting, and greenery, adding depth and warmth. Thoughtfully curated decor elements create a harmonious blend of style and function, resulting in a polished and welcoming environment suited for modern living.

PROJECT VIII.

BEDROOM REDECOR

The original bedroom was plain and lacked warmth, with minimal decor and limited visual appeal. Post-renovation, the space is revitalized with layered textures, including a plush velvet bench, rich blue accents, and varied textiles that add depth and sophistication. Thoughtfully selected artwork, lighting, and accessories create a cohesive, inviting atmosphere, transforming the room into a stylish and comfortable retreat.
